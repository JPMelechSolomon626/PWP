Milestone 1: PWP Purpose, Audience, and Goals

Purpose

The Purpose of this PWP is to aid me in my job search following completion of the program with an employer that meets my needs.

Audience

The audience would be prospective employers in the United States, Germany or Israel as well as individuals seeking a freelance multilingual developer for small one-shot projects.

Goals

The goals of my PWP constitute the following

  • To Provide a platform for publication of my digital resume
  • To garner interest in me for employment by prospective employers or potential clients by showcasing my skills and talents as they related to full stack development in their respective environments
  • To serve as a portfolio showing my various web projects, their functionality, their depth as a means to enhance interest in my web-development skills
  • To serve as a point of contact for prospective employers or potential clients

Persona: David Sachs: NGO Administrator

Name David Sachs
Role David runs a non-sectarian 3rd Party NGO that houses, educates cares for and fosters out homeless or displaced minor boys because of the ongoing conflicts in Israel and surrounding utilizing volunteers and aid groups to evacuate as many as possible.
Tech Bio David uses an HP Z-Book Fury G6 that he bought at "Topmarket" in Netanyah City. It's an 8-core processor with 64 gigs of RAM and a 3TB SSD.
Behaviors and Attitudes In terms of technology, David can best be described as a simple user. He has three universal standards
  • Can he turn it on
  • Does it do what he needs it to do without a large learning curve or time consumption
  • Does it meet his need?
Usage Scenario

David uses his website as a means to raise funds and take applications from potential foster parents. He also uses his site to feature members of his facilities both in the Israel,the US, Australia, the United Kingdom and Canada to generate interest in their plight in the hopes of seeing them fostered and adopted. He also uses his site as a means of encrypted communication between his organization and rescuers. He would also like to utilize his site as an internal secure site communication system, similar to reddit, where fosterlings can keep in touch with rescuers, parents and others for the purpose of eventual reunification between fosterlings and living relatives.

Frustrations and Limitations David is frustrated by the lack of multilingual web-developers out there who can meet his needs. His four biggest language needs are Arabic, English, Arcadian French and Hebrew. He would like to find a developer who has the resources and skills training to build the site and a system of linguists to aid in the translation of his site into the necessary languages.
Goals David's goals are simple.
  1. To have a site that can be utilized for fundraising, fostering, rescue and reunification.
  2. To use his site to advance awareness of minors who are the victims of conflict in the Middle East
  3. To use his site to facilitate communication between rescuers, waypoints and his organization through encrypted communication
  4. To facilitate the eventual reunification of fosters with biological families after the conflicts have resolved or once remaining family has made it to points of safety outside the conflict zone.
  5. To coordinate efforts between his organization and other 3rd party NGO's.
