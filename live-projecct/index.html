Marvin the melancholy robot, Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy, Douglas Adams

A "military brat" from birth, Josef grew up around punch cards and reel to reel computing. Over the course of his life has seen the world move from the Analog Age, through the Digital Age and finally into the Cyber Age. He has always had an interest in computers and computing learning Basic on the Commodore 64.

While an undergraduate at the University of Oklahoma Josef witnessed the birth of the internet. Shortly after he began hobby coding HTML by parsing code off Netscape of his favorite Sites.

After pursuing a two and a half decade career in public safety as a paramedic wildland firefighter and correctional officer, Josef decided to pursue a career in full stack development.
